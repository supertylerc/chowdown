ingredients:
- 1c Basmati Rice
- 2c Water
- 1 Tsp Salt
- 1 Tbsp Ghee

directions:
- Combine all ingredients into a pan and heat until simmering
- Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 25 minutes
- Remove lid and fluff Rice
