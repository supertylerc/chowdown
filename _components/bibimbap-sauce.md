ingredients:
- 2 Tbsp Gochujang (Korean red chili paste)
- 1 Tbsp Sesame Oil
- 1 Tbsp Brown Sugar
- 1 Tbsp Water
- 1 Tbsp Roasted Sesame Seeds
- 1 Tsp Apple Cider Vinegaro
- 1 Tsp Minced Garlic

directions:
- Combine all ingredients and whisk together

---

Sauce for either Beef Bibimbap or Chicken Bibimbap.
